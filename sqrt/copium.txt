Copium was first mined in the ancient Asgarthan mines, using the Hyperboreans as slaves... However Copium slowly refined and purified the Hyperborean genes and acted as a strong body stimulant making them much stronger... Eventually they revolted against the lizardly Asgarthans and slaughtered them escaping to an uninhabitated planet now called Earth... Some of the remaining Asgarthans in a last attempt at revenge infused their minds into captured Hyperboreans, their code name was Jew
