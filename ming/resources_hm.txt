Although **CEu Hard Mode** doesn't have DML for infinite resources, there are still many ways to get some raw material infinitely: (You won't need most of this after HV after you setup a robust oreproc)

- Electrolyze **Clay dust from Diorite** for aluminium, silicon, lithium, sodium, and small amounts of sulfur
- Electrolyze **Obsidian Dust** for Iron
- Farm **Ender Pearls** from EIO farming station
- ERF for thermal powders
- **Saltpeter** from Diorite
