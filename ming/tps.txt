Common cause for low tps (also check %t spark):
- Excessive use of:
  * conduits (see %t no_conduits)
  * facades
  * import / export buses
  * packagedauto
  * AA lasers
  * crafting cards
  * GT Pipes
  * Drawers
  * EIO Farming Stations (keep <5)

- Unfiltered exposer to a large network
- Large amount of entites (Make sure you void excess grains / sugarcane!)
- (CE) Amp cramming (multiple CEFs into 1 wire)
- Multiple drawer controllers (use controller slaves)
