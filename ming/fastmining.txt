How to mine an entire ore vein in 3min:
- Get a Efficiency 5. Unbreaking 3 (optional) Mining hammer
- Get a diamond hook and a void satchel (whitelist cobble/netherrack)
- (optional) Get a ZPM and a EIO sword with Direct from loot, put it in offhand for easy collecting 
- Mine straight down
- Mine until you find the edge of the vein
- Press [alt] -> array mining -> offset 3 -> count 10 
- Mining go zoom zoom (make sure to check the durability of the hammer)
- Use the hook to move around and collect the ores
